# InsightVR CRM — Deployment Guide Follow these steps in order. Each takes only a few minutes. --- ## Step 1 — Create your Supabase database (free) 1. Go to **https://supabase.com** and click "Start your project" 2. Sign up with your email or Google account 3. Click **"New project"** and fill in: - Name: `insightvr-crm` - Database password: choose something strong and save it somewhere - Region: pick the closest to you (e.g. US East) 4. Wait ~2 minutes for the project to be created 5. Once ready, go to the left sidebar → **SQL Editor** 6. Click **"New query"**, then paste the entire contents of `supabase-setup.sql` into the editor 7. Click **"Run"** — you should see "Success" 8. Now go to **Settings → API** in the sidebar 9. Copy and save two values: - **Project URL** (looks like `https://abcdefgh.supabase.co`) - **anon / public key** (long string starting with `eyJ...`) --- ## Step 2 — Put your code on GitHub (free) 1. Go to **https://github.com** and sign up for a free account 2. Click the **"+"** in the top right → **"New repository"** 3. Name it `insightvr-crm`, set it to **Public**, click **"Create repository"** 4. On the next screen, click **"uploading an existing file"** 5. Drag and drop just the **`index.html`** file (not the SQL file — that was only needed once) 6. Click **"Commit changes"** --- ## Step 3 — Deploy to Vercel (free, gives you a real URL) 1. Go to **https://vercel.com** and sign up with your GitHub account 2. Click **"Add New Project"** 3. You'll see your `insightvr-crm` repo — click **"Import"** 4. Leave all settings as default and click **"Deploy"** 5. Wait about 30 seconds — Vercel will give you a URL like: `https://insightvr-crm.vercel.app` 6. That's your permanent CRM link! Bookmark it and share it with your team. --- ## Step 4 — Connect to your database (first time only) 1. Open your Vercel URL in the browser 2. You'll see a setup screen asking for your Supabase credentials 3. Paste in the **Project URL** and **anon key** you saved in Step 1 4. Click **"Connect & Launch CRM"** 5. Done! Your CRM is live. > **Note for your team:** Each team member only needs to do Step 4 once on their device. The credentials are saved in their browser automatically after that. --- ## Future updates If you ever want to change something in the CRM, just: 1. Edit `index.html` 2. Go to your GitHub repo → click `index.html` → click the pencil (edit) icon 3. Paste in the new code and commit 4. Vercel automatically redeploys in ~30 seconds --- ## Your CRM includes - **Dashboard** — live metrics, renewal alerts, overdue evaluation warnings - **Sales pipeline** — Prospect → Intro meeting → Early diligence → Late diligence → Proposal sent → Deal won/lost - **Implementation pipeline** — Discovery & planning → Go-live scheduled → First 30 days → First 60 days → Overdue - **Active accounts** — kit count, hardware cost, subscription type/amount, renewal tracking - **Renewals** — accounts bucketed by urgency with color-coded warnings - **Full account records** — contact info, implementation details, subscription fields, notes --- Questions? Ask Claude to help you troubleshoot any step.
